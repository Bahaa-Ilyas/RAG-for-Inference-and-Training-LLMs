SOC2 Control CC6.2 - Logical Access Review

Quarterly user access reviews are required for production, finance, and customer support systems.

Evidence must include reviewer identity, exported access list, exception tickets, remediation owner, and sign-off timestamp.

If evidence is missing, Compliance Operations must be notified within five business days.
