# Master Services Agreement MSA-2026

Vendor: Vendor Atlas

## Section 8.2 Liability Cap

Aggregate liability is limited to two times the trailing twelve-month fees paid under the agreement.

The cap does not apply to confidentiality breaches, data protection violations, unpaid fees, or gross negligence. Legal Operations must review the liability cap clause before signature.
