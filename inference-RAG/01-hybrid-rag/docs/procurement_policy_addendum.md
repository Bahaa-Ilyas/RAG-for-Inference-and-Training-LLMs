# Procurement Addendum PR-14

## High-Risk Vendor Escalation

High-risk vendors must be escalated when one or more of these conditions apply:

- Annual spend exceeds 250000 USD.
- The vendor processes regulated customer data.
- The vendor has unresolved critical security findings.

Escalation goes to the Risk Committee and must occur before purchase order approval.
